The Fast Food

Fast food restaurants are traditionally distinguished by the drive-through. The Fast Food

Many fast foods tend to be high in saturated fat, sugar, salt and calories.[5] Fast food has been linked to increased risk of cardiovascular disease, colorectal cancer, obesity, high cholesterol, insulin resistance conditions and depression.[6][7][8][9][10] These correlations remain strong even when controlling for confounding lifestyle variables, suggesting a strong association between fast food consumption and increased risk of disease and early mortality.
